The Visionary Behind AVM

Late Satya Narayan Bahadur Shrestha founded Adarsha Vidya Mandir with a revolutionary vision: to make quality education accessible to all sections of society, regardless of their economic background.

His philosophy of "Be Good, Do Good" became the cornerstone of AVM's educational approach, emphasizing not just academic excellence but also character development and social responsibility.

Under his leadership, AVM grew from a small institution to one of Nepal's most respected educational establishments, setting new standards in holistic education and student development.

"Education is not just about acquiring knowledge; it's about becoming a better human being and contributing positively to society."

- Late Satya Narayan Bahadur Shrestha

Campus Evolution
